I am a freelance illustrator based in lovely Netherlands. Loving people, animals and nature, inspired by them all. I believe imperfection and distortion has beauty in it. We love finding joy in imperfect world of The moody society. In this colorful illustration world each The moody society character represents a different set of feeling, values and moments of life. Appreciating conscious consumption of resources, using environment friendly materials and collaborating with local artisans. Happy to see you here visiting me and looking forward to hear from you! Lots of love, Gozde
